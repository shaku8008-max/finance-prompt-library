# Customer Service Workflow

This section automates complaint handling and internal escalation processes.

---

## Workflow Overview

1. P06 — Complaint Triage  
2. P07 — Response Draft  
3. P08 — Escalation Summary  

---

## Business Value

- Faster response times  
- Consistent customer communication  
- Improved complaint routing  

---

## Key Outputs

- Complaint classifications  
- Response drafts  
- Escalation summaries  

---

## Risks

- Misclassification of complaints  
- Inappropriate responses  

Mitigation:
- Human review for high-risk cases  
- Tone and constraint controls
