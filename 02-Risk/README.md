# Risk Analysis Workflow

This section focuses on identifying and prioritising risks based on compliance outputs.

---

## Workflow Overview

1. P04 — Risk Identification  
2. P05 — Risk Prioritisation  

---

## Business Value

- Faster risk detection  
- Consistent risk classification  
- Improved decision-making  

---

## Key Outputs

- Risk lists  
- Risk rankings  
- Priority scores  

---

## Risks

- Incorrect risk prioritisation  
- Over-reliance on automated scoring  

Mitigation:
- Human validation for high-priority risks  
- Structured scoring framework
