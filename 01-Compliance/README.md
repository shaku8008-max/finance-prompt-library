# Compliance Workflow

This section focuses on transforming regulatory documents into structured compliance outputs.

---

## Workflow Overview

1. P01 — Document Summary  
2. P02 — Policy Extraction  
3. P03 — Compliance Checklist  

---

## Business Value

- Reduces document review time by up to 90%  
- Standardises compliance interpretation  
- Improves audit readiness  

---

## Key Outputs

- Document summaries  
- Policy rules  
- Compliance checklists  

---

## Risks

- Missing critical regulatory details  
- Misinterpretation of policies  

Mitigation:
- Human review for high-risk documents  
- Structured output constraints
